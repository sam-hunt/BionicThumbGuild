## About

The Bionic Thumb Guild is proud to present its flagship product: **the Bionic Thumb**.

"But it's a whole hand," you say. Yes. The Guild knows. They just really, really care about thumbs. Every servo, joint, and actuator in the other four fingers exists to support the thumb's extraordinary polyarthric range of motion. The Guild's engineers insist the name is accurate — the thumb _is_ the hand, the rest is just scaffolding.

This mod adds the Bionic Thumb as a hand prosthetic with 120% manipulation efficiency. Orbital traders stock them automatically, because the Guild's distribution network is vast and its marketing department has priorities.

## Features

- **Bionic Thumb** - A full bionic hand prosthetic with 120% manipulation efficiency, named "bionic thumb" because the Guild's marketing department has priorities
- **Surgical Installation** - Your doctor will not question the name
- **Orbital Trader Stock** - All orbital traders carry 2x Bionic Thumbs in their inventory. The Guild's sales reps are everywhere. Do not ask how they got aboard your orbital trader

## Requirements

- **RimWorld 1.6** or later
- **Harmony** (auto-download from Steam Workshop if you don't have it)
- No DLC required

## Installation

### Steam Workshop (Recommended)

Subscribe on the Steam Workshop and it will auto-download.

### Manual Installation

1. Download the latest release from the [Releases](https://github.com/sam-hunt/BionicThumbGuild/releases) page
2. Extract the `BionicThumbGuild` folder to your RimWorld `Mods` directory:
   - **Windows**: `C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\RimWorld\Mods\`
   - **Mac**: `~/Library/Application Support/Steam/steamapps/common/RimWorld/RimWorldMac.app/Mods/`
   - **Linux**: `~/.steam/steam/steamapps/common/RimWorld/Mods/`
3. Enable the mod in RimWorld's mod menu
4. Restart RimWorld

## Compatibility

- **Safe to add** to existing saves. The Guild welcomes new members at any time.
- **Safe to remove** from saves, as long as no colonists currently have a bionic thumb installed. The Guild respects your decision to leave, but will be disappointed. Failure to comply may result in repossession.
- Should be compatible with most mods. No vanilla content is overwritten. The Guild plays well with others.
