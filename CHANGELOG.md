## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-15

Initial release of Bionic Thumb Guild for RimWorld 1.6.

### Added

- **Bionic Thumb** prosthetic body part with 120% manipulation efficiency
- Surgical installation recipe (installs on hands — the Guild insists they're thumbs)
- Orbital traders now stock 2x Bionic Thumbs via Harmony postfix patch
- Requires [Harmony](https://github.com/pardeike/HarmonyRimWorld); no DLC required
